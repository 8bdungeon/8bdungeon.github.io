8-Bitty Dungeon

A rogue-like dungeon crawler


8-bitty Dungeon was made for the TinyCircuits Thumby.




If you don't have Thumby, you can still play via the
Thumby Arcade.

Enemies


You'll encounter two enemies in the dungeons,
and they aren't going to leave a room alone just
because you have run them all off!

Goblins



Goblins are everywhere in these dungeons.

Minotaurs



Minotaurs are much stronger than goblins,
but also much more rare (and richer).

Landmarks


You'll encounter two landmarks in the dungeons,
and enemies will never be present in these rooms
nor the first room you enter on a level.

Encountering either refills your health.

Shops



Shops allow you to purchase upgrades.

Stairs



Entering stairs is how you descend further
into the dungeon.
