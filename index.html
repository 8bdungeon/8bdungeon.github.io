8-Bitty Dungeon

A rogue-like dungeon crawler


8-bitty Dungeon was made for the TinyCircuits Thumby.




If you don't have Thumby, you can still play via the
Thumby Arcade.

Enemies


You'll encounter two enemies in the dungeons, and they aren't going to leave a room alone just because you have run them all off!

Goblins



Goblins are everywhere. You can blaime Tolkein for that.

Minotaurs



Minotaurs are much stronger than goblins, but also much more rare (and they're rich!).

Landmarks


These are safe areas; enemies don't spawn here (nor in the first room of a level). Entering either one of the following refills your health.

Shops



Buy upgrades, get strong.

Stairs



Explore a new level of the dungeon.
